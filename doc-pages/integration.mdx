## Integration

<Card>
  Ello is built to connect with your stack. Use no-code connectors or deep integrations.

  #### **No-code & Workflow**

  Connect via Zapier, Make, or n8n to trigger actions across thousands of SaaS apps.

  #### **Developer Integrations**

  Use the MCP server for secure, performant integration into backend systems, or call REST APIs and webhooks directly.

  #### **Planned Direct Integrations**

  Ello plans direct integrations with many productivity and data platforms such as Google Suite, GitHub, Slack, Salesforce, Shopify, Zendesk, and more.
</Card>
